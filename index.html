JointDreamer: Joint Score Distillation for Text-to-3D Generation via Universal Guidance Model

Paper Project Gallery Code Coming Soon

Abstract

Score Distillation Sampling (SDS) has shown great promise in text-to-3D generation by distilling the pre-trained 2D image distribution to the rendered image distribution from a 3D representation. However, we reveal that SDS conducts distillation on each view independently, lacking awareness of coherence across views and inheriting the biased 2D image distribution, resulting in inconsistent 3D scenes.

In this work, we propose Joint Score Distillation (JSD), a new rule of score distillation to recover a consistent 3D scene. Specifically, we re-formulate the score distillation jointly across multiple views, guided by the coherency across these views. On top of that, we present an efficient yet effective coherency binary classifier that can serve as a guidance source. We further leverage other guidance models, demonstrating that JSD can seamlessly integrate with universal guidance models to enhance geometric consistency.

Our overall approach, named JointDreamer, is capable of generating highly 3D consistent and high-fidelity 3D assets through various 3D representations, including NeRF and DMTet. JointDreamer sets a new state-of-the-art performance standard, as supported by both qualitative and quantitative evaluations.

Joint Score Distillation

Our JSD extend the Score Distillation to include multiple views. This fosters coherence between different views of 3D representation, promoting convergence toward a 3D-consistent representation.
